Common Questions

Before you decide

What data do I need to provide?
At minimum, a 30-day CSV export from your wearable device (WHOOP, Oura, Apple Watch, Garmin, or Fitbit), plus your age, height, weight, and goals. For Deep Dive and Protocol Build, 90+ days of data and your supplement list, training schedule, and bloodwork (if available) give the most actionable results.
How do I export my wearable data?
After you submit the intake form, I'll email you step-by-step instructions specific to your device. WHOOP: Profile → Settings → Data Export. Oura: cloud.ouraring.com → Settings → Export. Apple Watch: use the Health Auto Export app. It takes about 2 minutes.
Who actually analyzes my data?
Me — Zach Czemeres. Ranked #97 globally on Don't Die, functional age 23.5, 41 biomarkers with zero out of range. I analyze your data personally using the same tools and approach I use for my own protocol. This is not automated software.
Is this medical advice?
No. This is data interpretation and lifestyle optimization. I'll tell you what your data shows and what changes could improve your metrics based on my experience and research. Always consult a doctor for medical decisions.
Can I cancel Protocol Build anytime?
Yes. Cancel anytime through Stripe — no questions asked. Your existing dashboards stay accessible through the client portal. There's no contract or commitment.
What if I'm not satisfied?
If your dashboard doesn't deliver actionable insights, email me and I'll make it right — whether that's a revision or a full refund. My name is on every analysis. I don't ship work I wouldn't want for myself.

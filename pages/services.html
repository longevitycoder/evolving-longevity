What Gets Analyzed

The patterns your app won't show you

◎

Sleep Architecture

Deep sleep duration and consistency, REM quality, overnight RHR patterns, sleep latency, and what's suppressing your recovery overnight. Not just a score — the why behind it.

⟡

Recovery Patterns

What's actually tanking your recovery: alcohol timing, late meals, overtraining, stress cycles, or something you haven't identified yet. Pattern detection across your full dataset.

△

Training Correlation

How your workout strain maps to next-day recovery. Are you training on red days? Undertrained on green? I'll show you the mismatch and how to fix your timing.

◇

HRV Trend Analysis

Your HRV baseline, variance, and trajectory over time. What's driving your best days and your worst. The single most important metric for long-term health — decoded.

▣

Supplement Audit

Your current stack reviewed against clinical dosing research. What to keep, what to cut, and what you might be missing — based on your data, not generic advice.

◈

Bloodwork Integration

If you provide blood panel results, I'll cross-reference them with your wearable data to find connections your doctor won't look for. (Deep Dive and above.)
